# Canna Cookies

## Equipment

- oven
- stove
- sauce pan
- baking sheet or foil

## Ingredients

- 1g hash
- 115g butter
- 150g milk
- 150g sugar
- 30g coco powder
- 450g oats
- 120g peanut butter
- vanilla to taste
